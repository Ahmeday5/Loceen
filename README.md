# Loceen Dashboard

**Loceen Dashboard** is an enterprise-level admin dashboard designed for **drilling and mining companies**, providing a centralized system to manage operational, financial, and administrative data.

The platform focuses on **financial tracking and internal management**, offering a lightweight accounting system that connects all company activities into a single dashboard.

The frontend is built using **Angular**, with backend integration planned using **.NET Core**.

---

## 🧩 System Overview

Loceen is designed to help drilling and mining companies manage complex operations by unifying multiple business domains such as finance, operations, and human resources.

All modules are interconnected to ensure accurate reporting and financial consistency.

---

## 🏗️ Core Modules

### 🔹 Sales Management
- Track sales operations
- Link sales with financial records
- Revenue monitoring

### 🔹 Suppliers Management
- Manage suppliers and purchase-related data
- Supplier financial tracking

### 🔹 Employees & Workforce
- Employee records management
- Job roles and assignments
- Salary structures

### 🔹 Payroll System
- Monthly salaries calculation
- Allowances and deductions
- Payroll history

### 🔹 Expenses & Revenues
- Register expenses and incomes
- Categorized financial records
- Cash flow tracking

### 🔹 Equipment Management
- Drilling and mining equipment records
- Equipment-related expenses
- Usage tracking

### 🔹 Rentals & Leases
- Equipment and asset rental tracking
- Rental periods and costs

### 🔹 Financial Ratios & Analytics
- Profitability ratios
- Cost analysis
- Financial performance indicators

### 🔹 Accounting & Reports
- Basic accounting system
- Financial reports and summaries
- Custom queries and data analysis
